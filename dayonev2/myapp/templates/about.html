About Us

Welcome to DayONE a web app designed to boost your productivity and help you make the most of each day!

We're a small but passionate team, led by Sammengi Arjun, a 2nd-year student at IIEST, Shibpur. Sammengi Arjun is passionate about creating value-driven products with simple and user-friendly designs. We're committed to helping you achieve your goals and make every day productive. If you have any feedback or suggestions, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at sammengiarjun@gmail.com. We value your input!
